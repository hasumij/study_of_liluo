游戏攻略

注：除特殊说明（由括号标注）外，按照顺序点击即可解锁对应的结局

  1. examine workbench
    • remove piece of metal – bed end 1– An Unwise Decision
  2. remove high-heeled shoes with piece of metal
  3. remove leather sharps with feet
  4. open workbench
  5. remove chain with wrench
    • remove armbinder with hooks（在拿到梯子（ladder）之前执行此操作均可）– bad end 2 – Hung Out to Dry
  6. move wheeled cart to hooks
    • open shed door with feet（在双手被拘束，戴着口球（ballgag）时执行此操作均可） – bad end 6 – An Easy Target
  7. move wheeled cart to barred window
    • open wooden door with key from sill（在没有摘下口球（ballgag）时用钥匙（key from sill）做任何事都可）– bad end 3 – A Simple Mistake
  8. open cabinet with key under cart
    • move harness ballgag with hooks inside cabinet – bad end 4 – A Big Crash (Variant A)
  9. move tools
  10. remove harness ballgag with hooks inside the cabinet
    • remove armbinder with hooks inside the cabinet（在单手套（armbinder）在时的任何时候执行此操作都可） – bad end 5 – A Big Crash (Variant B)
  11. open wooden door with key from sill
    • open shed door with feet（在双手被拘束时在时执行此操作均可） – bad end 7 – A Rebellion Extinguished
  12. move stepladder to hooks
  13. remove armbinder with hooks
  14. open small toolbox
  15. remove wrist and elbow ropes with utility knife
  16. move bulletin board
  17. move utility knife to hole in the wall
  18. remove elbow ropes with utility knife
    • open shed door with feet（在拘束带（leather harness）和项圈（collar）都在时在时执行此操作均可） – bad end 8 – An Uphill Battle (Variant A)
    • open shed door with feet（在项圈（collar）在时在时执行此操作均可） – bad end 9 – An Uphill Battle (Variant B)
    • open shed door with feet（在拘束带（leather harness）在时在时执行此操作均可） – bad end 10 – A Stimulating Experience
  19. open drawers
  20. remove collar with bolt cutters
  21. move stepladder to high shelf
  22. remove leather with wire cutters
    • 执行其他任意操作（检查（examine）不算） – bad end 11 – Into Loving Arms
  23. open shed door with feet
  24. happy end – Business as Usual
